Homer Simpson

Early Life

Homer Simpson was born on May 12th, 1956.
He is the only child of the marriage of Abraham J. Simpson and Mona Simpson who for most of his life believed to be have died when he was still a boy.
C. Montgomery Burns, Homer's boss, is also Homer's distant relative according to the Simpson Family Tree from "The Simpsons Uncensored Family Album."
