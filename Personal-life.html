Homer Simpson

Personal Life


Homer Jay Simpson, also known as just Homer, is a husband, a father of 3 kids, a worker, and most importantly, an icon in the animated franchise. Him and his wife, Marge Simpson, have been married for 29, almost 30 years now. He has 3 kids, the oldest being bart Simpson who is 10 years old in the show, the middle child being Lisa Simpson who is 8 years old, and the youngest being Maggie Simpson, who is only 1 year old.
Homer works at a 9-5 job as a low-level safety insperctor at the Springfild Nuclear Power plant.
He always is either at work, at home taking care of his wife and kids, or at Moe's Tavern drinking a beer.
